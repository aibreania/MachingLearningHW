Q21: Given that

$$

What is the value of ${\bf A}^{-1} {\bf A} {\bf B}$?

Because ${\bf A}^{-1} {\bf A} equals to matrix with diagonal 1s, and diagonal 1s multiplies B is still B, thus the result is the same as B.
